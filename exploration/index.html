Exploration

NE PLC has engaged leading international experts to conduct and supervise the exploration project at Jambi.  Major contributors have included Wardell Armstrong from the UK, Britmindo co-ordinating the project from Indonesia, with Boart Longyear and Connell Wagner conducting drilling and logistics respectively. 

The main exploration programme ran from March to September 2008, with a follow-up programme running in January and February 2009.
